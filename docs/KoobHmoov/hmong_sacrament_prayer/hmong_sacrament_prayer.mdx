# Sacrament Prayer in Hmong

Here is a voice recording in Hmong for the sacrament prayer for both water and bread.

## Bread

<audio controls>
  <source src="/voice/bread.mp3" type="audio/mpeg" />
  Your browser does not support the audio element.
</audio>

Auj Vajtswv, Leej Txiv tus uas Nyob Mus Ib Txhis, peb thov koj ntawm koj Leej Tub, Yexus Khetos lub npe, kom koj foom koob hmoov thiab ua kom cov mov mog no dawb huv rau txhua leej txhua tus uas noj, kom lawv yuav noj nco txog koj Leej Tub lub cev, thiab ua tim khawv rau koj, auj Vajtswv, Leej Txiv tus uas Nyob Mus Ib Txhis, hais tias lawv txaus siab ris koj Leej Tub lub npe, thiab nco ntsoov txog nws thiab ua raws li nws tej lus txib uas nws tau muab rau lawv; kom lawv yuav muaj nws tus Ntsuj Plig nrog lawv nyob tas mus li. Amees.

Auj Vajtswv, Leej Txiv tus uas Nyob Mus Ib Txhis, peb thov koj ntawm koj Leej Tub, Yexus Khetos lub npe, kom koj foom koob hmoov thiab ua kom cov dej no dawb huv rau txhua leej txhua tus uas haus, kom lawv yuav haus nco txog koj Leej Tub cov ntshav, uas tau nrog theej lawv; kom lawv yuav ua tim khawv rau koj, auj Vajtswv, Leej Txiv tus uas Nyob Mus Ib Txhis, hais tias lawv nco ntsoov txog nws, kom lawv yuav muaj nws tus Ntsuj Plig nrog lawv nyob. Amees.
